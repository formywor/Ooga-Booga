FAQ
The Stripe service is kept separate so checkout logic does not mix with your launcher/session API.
How does checkout work?
When you click buy, this page sends your selected plan to the Stripe service on stripe.scriptnovaa.com. That service creates a Stripe Checkout Session and sends you to secure hosted checkout.
Will keys be delivered automatically?
This page is ready for automatic delivery. The separate Stripe API should create the order, listen for the successful checkout webhook, generate the key, and show it on the success page.
Why separate the Stripe API?
Keeping payments on a separate subdomain helps keep Stripe secrets, webhooks, checkout sessions, and purchase logic away from the launcher/session API.
Do yearly plans really save money?
Yes. The yearly pricing is set to save about 15% compared with paying monthly for a full year.
Who can buy EXPRESS?
EXPRESS is meant for serious use, teams, and larger projects. It is limited to ScriptNova developers or people who own a gaming website when released.
What about BLACK EXPRESS?
BLACK EXPRESS is the top one-time tier. It is meant for larger serious use cases and may require extra review or eligibility approval before full activation.
