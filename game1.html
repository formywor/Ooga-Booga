NEON GAUNTLET

A hard twin-stick survival arena game built for controller play. Dodge swarms, chain score multipliers, trigger perfect dashes, survive elite waves, collect random power-ups, crack reward crates, beat the boss, then keep going in endless mode.

How to play

  • Move with left stick or WASD.
  • Aim with right stick or mouse. Fire automatically while aiming.
  • Dash through danger. Perfect dashes boost damage and score.
  • Bomb clears bullets and enemies around you.
  • Build streak and multiplier by killing fast and not getting hit.
  • Every 3 waves you get upgrade choices.
  • Power-ups can drop during fights and from reward crates.
  • Wave 9 begins the boss fight. After that it becomes endless.

Controls

  • Controller: Left Stick move, Right Stick aim, A dash, B bomb, Start pause
  • Keyboard: WASD move, Mouse aim, Space dash, Shift bomb, Esc pause
  • Restart: Enter on game over or press A
  • High score: saved in your browser
