Support

Welcome to the support page. This page explains how the app works, how to manage blocked users, and where to find important settings.

How the app works

The app is designed to help you communicate and manage your experience more easily. Depending on the feature you are using, the app may let you send messages, view content, manage contacts, and update your settings.

  • Open the app and sign in to access your account features.
  • Use the main screen to view your activity, messages, or other available tools.
  • Use the settings area to control privacy, account, and blocked user options.

Blocking a user

If you no longer want someone to contact you or interact with you in the app, you can block them.

  • Open the user’s profile or the conversation with that user.
  • Select the available options menu.
  • Choose Block User.
  • Once blocked, that user will no longer be able to interact with you in the normal way supported by the app.

Unblocking a user

To unblock someone, go to Manage Blocked Users in Settings.

  • Open Settings.
  • Select Manage Blocked Users.
  • Find the person you want to unblock.
  • Choose Unblock next to their name.

After unblocking, that person may be able to contact or interact with you again based on how the app works.

Settings and account help

  • Go to Settings to update your preferences.
  • Use Settings to manage blocked users, account details, and privacy options.
  • If something is not working correctly, try fully closing and reopening the app.
  • Make sure you are using the latest version of the app.

Troubleshooting

  • If messages or content do not load, check your internet connection.
  • If a blocked or unblocked user change does not appear right away, refresh the app or reopen it.
  • If you cannot access a feature, confirm that you are signed in to the correct account.
